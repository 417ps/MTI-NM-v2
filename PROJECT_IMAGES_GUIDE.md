## Recommended Image Specifications:

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Resolution:** 1200x800px minimum
- **Aspect Ratio:** 3:2 (landscape)
- **File Size:** Under 500KB (optimized for web)
- **Style:** Professional, industrial, government-appropriate
