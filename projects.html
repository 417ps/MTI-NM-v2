Project Challenge

The SIGMA facility represents one of the most challenging construction environments in the nation - a high-security weapons facility requiring specialized security clearances, extensive safety protocols, and compliance with hazardous material regulations. The electrical infrastructure required complete modernization across five distinct phases while maintaining operational security and facility function.

MTI's Solution

MTI successfully manages 2 of the 3 current design-build projects within the SIGMA facility, leveraging our specialized security training and proven hazardous material protocols. Our partnership with Pueblo Electric ensures technical excellence while our project management expertise handles the complex coordination requirements.
